T, XO

Passion through music

Welcome, and thank you for visiting! My name is T XO, but you can call me Teddi. As an independent artist straddling the genres of alternative, punk-pop, and country, I fuse raw emotion with evocative storytelling. My musical journey began in the harmonious surroundings of church and school choirs, which instilled in me a profound way to connect and express myself through song. My unique sound blends the spirited energy of punk-pop with the soulful depths of country, encompassing everything from electrifying anthems to poignant ballads.
